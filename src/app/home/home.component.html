There are three major non-profit organizations in the UK. Their main purpose is to look after historic properties for the next generations.

  • National Trust (NT)

    With over 500 properties.

    Mainly castles, houses, buildings and gardens.

  • English Heritage (EH)

    With over 450 properties.

    Mainly castles, ruins, houses and buildings.

  • Historic Houses Association (HHA)

    With over 1600 buildings. 500 of them are available for public viewing.

    Mainly private houses and gardens.
